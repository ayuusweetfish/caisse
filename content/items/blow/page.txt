Team entry to <link https://globalgamejam.org Global Game Jam> 2025, with the theme <it Bubble>. Took care of game design (collaborative), music/sound effects, and technical development.

<link https://blow.ayu.land Play here>.

A draw-and-guess game with bubbles as the paintbrush. The player drags and pops the soap bubbles to paint, while the cyber kitten attempts to figure out these bubbly doodles. How can just three bubbles capture the vastness of the world? We believe that every player will find inspirations of their own. Bubbles always remind us of our past childhood memories; here, we try to recreate a slice of a mellow afternoon from the 2000s in pixel art.

!<img photo_en.png <= Game screenshot, an “Orange” painted by bubbles is guessed correctly by the kitten; confetti fly in the air.> vertical small>

Post-jam update: bubble highlight; English support; extended word list; screenshot. The image recognition model was also migrated from Gemini 1.5 Flash (which had already been retired) to the newly-released <link https://qwen.ai/blog?id=qwen3.5 Qwen 3.5>. <date 26.02,26.03>
