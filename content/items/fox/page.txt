与其说是游戏，其实更像是玩具。人工语言模型的“想象力”在这一年渐趋成熟，词元序列里天马行空的想法开始有了形状，于是我试着在其中刻画出了一片不着边际的随机梦话海洋。那个时候 <link https://www.deepseek.com/ DeepSeek> 还没有被世人所熟知，调试时的 V3 模型跑得飞快；我想，这只鲸鱼漫游的大概确实是一片孤独的海吧。做到一半时发布了一个新模型，我试了试，感觉这堆不知所云的东西并不需要太多额外的思考，故没有选择迁移。这个模型便是后来大名鼎鼎的 R1，几天后就随着官方客户端的发布火遍整片大陆——而这片刚发现的海洋也跟着干涸了好多好多天……

Inquiring minds alike, Fox Newroll Network (FoxNN) has you covered! In the 22nd century, foxes are the playful super-wizards. They traverse the world on a daily basis, observing, and discovering through a mechanism known as “heads or tails” — no, it’s not coin flipping, but a secret fox magic outside of the reach of languages~

Bring your cup of moonbeam tea, join the foxes in their inquiries for this issue!

!<hr>

This is more of a toy than a game. This was the year when the “imagination” of artificial language models grew towards maturity; the flying ideas in the token sequences started to take shape. So, I tried to cartograph a boundless ocean in it, composed of random daydreams. It was a time when <link https://www.deepseek.com/ DeepSeek> was not known to all, the V3 model running lightning fast during debugging. This whale was probably indeed wandering a lonely ocean, I thought.

Halfway through the jam, a new model was released. I tried it, but felt that this pile of ungrounded murmurs did not call for much additional thinking, and chose not to migrate. This model was the later famous R1, which, with the release of the official client just a few days later, became phenomenal across the land — while drying this newly discovered ocean up for quite a few days…

One year later, the image model was migrated from the original Tongyi Wanx to <link https://github.com/Tongyi-MAI/Z-Image Z-Image-Turbo>, a lightweight model recently released, deployed on a server shared by our research lab. This not only reduced the consumption of computing resources, but also achieved a more consistent visual style [and protected my wallet! <kao (๑ゝω╹๑)>]. Also, all the processing involved by this time was run on open weights and open source programs. <date 2026.02>
