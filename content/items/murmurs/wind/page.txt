!<note Return to <link murmurs>>
<lang zh 军韵> (<it Rhymes of a Marching Band>; author’s translation) is an internal journal of the Tsinghua University Symphonic Band, a part of the university’s student art troupe. The journal is published biannually, accepting submissions from all members of the band. Submissions made are collected here.
<it Wind Vibes> is a Chinese interpretation of a musical piece title literally translated back into English. The phrase is also frequently referenced in performances and relevant activities, thus borrowed here.
