The parts responsible for page content are written in the Lua language; the daemons on the cloud have their incantation in JavaScript and currently live on the <link https://deno.land/ Deno Deploy> platform. The <link https://github.com/ayuusweetfish/caisse source repository> can be accessed on GitHub.
