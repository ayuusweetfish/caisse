本次仍然负责全部电子技术实现部分；美术设计来自莓辣成员 Annie。相比 <link maylove-ex-22 2022 年版本>，本次将技术方案改为电容传感，实现完整而美观的展板表面。电路集成在印刷电路板上，大部分元件在展览现场完成组装测试。装置搭建过程中获得了莓辣成员、展览志愿者与外援们的大量协助。谢谢 Sea、Milky、Annie、海涵、77、海子、花酱、肥肥、加盐、妹妹、小李（各组内按出场时间顺序）以及许多没有留下名字的大家——

An interactive installation in the 2023 sex-ed exhibition, <it I Have Asked Many People About You>, by MAYLOVE, Guizhou Huachengxinghe Culture Co. Ltd., and PLUCK. The exhibition venue is at Wonderful Square, Guiyang.
!<img Signpost.jpg <= Themed signpost on the outdoor ground of the square.>>

Reach out the hands and light up the body parts on the “body textbook”. Observe the different colours of the lights, and see the children’s understandings of privacy regarding their own bodies.

This time I was again responsible for the implementation of electronic technologies. Artwork design by MAYLOVE member Annie. Compared to the <link maylove-ex-22 2022 revision>, this one changes the technical approach to capacitive sensing, resulting in a coherent, aesthetically pleasing display surface. The circuit is integrated on a printed circuit board, with the majority of components assembled and tested on-site. Throughout the setup process, tremendous aid was received from the MAYLOVE members, exhibition volunteers, and external participants. Gratitude goes to Sea, Milky, Annie, Haihan, Qiqi, Haizi, Huajiang, Feifei, Jiayan, the little sister, and Li (in order of appearance within each group) together with a lot of friends who did not leave behind a name.
