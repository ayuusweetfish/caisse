信息时代里待得久了，都开始觉得从前人与人的交情实在奇妙。苏东坡赴京赶考之后，也不知什么时候二人才能再次见面；和尚对于东坡而言成了虚幻的记忆与文字，于是所见的每一座青山似乎都有佛印和尚。见到真正的和尚，便仿佛与青山重叠，而和尚的佛性又好似无言的大自然一般，二者浑然一体——

A story between the ancient Chinese poet Su Dongpo and the monk Fo-Yin. The famous pun lines “狗啃河上骨”, “水流东坡诗” — “Dog eating a bone on the river, water carrying a poem east of the slope”, which, based on the pronunciation, can also be interpreted as “dog eating the bones of the monk, water carrying the corpse of Dongpo” — are a joke between the two. Of course, the depth of their friendship goes far beyond a song or a paragraph of text.
Immersed in the information age, one starts to be fascinated by people’s relationships in the past. After Su Dongpo went to the capital for his exam, it would no more be clear when they would meet again; for Dongpo, the monk became an unreal blur of memory and language, and then every hill he saw seemed to have the monk in it. Should he eventually meet the real Fo-Yin, the monk would start to overlap with the hills, with the monk’s Buddhist insights blending into the wordless nature, unifying the two —
At the end of the day, however, none of these debates matter. It is Su Dongpo’s carefree spirit — “who cares that it’s difficult to be together”, “find peace in wherever one is placed” — that is the most touching.

It was a few days before my twentieth birthday. As I watched the sunset shining through the window, I wondered whether I’d ever have the chance to experience such pure and beautiful emotions myself. Back then, I had no idea of love, life plans, or anything. I simply longed for the mellow taste and did not want to leave any regret. Yet, I was at a loss to go forward. “In the sky after a summer rain, building a wide rainbow because I like you.” As if right in front of me were a scene of a soul rushing to embrace another; can it be any more romantic? Then, tears mixed with bittersweet emotions and sadness ran down in the warm shadows cast by the glass windows.
Actually, the duo were only 23 when they wrote this song — it was hard for me then to believe that they could pursue such ideals and create such touching works right after graduating from university, as all these seemed to belong to mature adults. But really, twenty years of life can already be rich enough; and who doesn’t have the choice to revert to being a child again sometimes?
Soon afterwards, there was a mutual attraction that turned into a relationship. Humming this song through the four seasons, and carefully copying the melodies onto paper — isn’t life magical?
