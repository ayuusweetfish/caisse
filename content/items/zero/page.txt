!<h1 Concoction<date 2018.04>>

其实是鬼畜乐曲哒！
Coursera 课程<link https://www.coursera.org/learn/audio-signal-processing 《音乐应用的音频信号处理》>结课作业。作业要求从 <link https://freesound.org/ Freesound> 上寻找声音，并使用课程提供的 Python 软件包进行信号处理操作（如频谱拆分、移调等），在此基础上创作一首乐曲。
提交作业原标题为《Kitchraensform》。说明文档中解释了制作过程，原始文件包包含了音频素材与所用的程序代码。

!<h1 Adornment<date 2017.24>>

In the final winter holiday of high school I finally decided to start making music on my own. I pieced together some electronic music with the only things I had at the time — bundled synthesizers in LMMS and drum samples obtained from <link https://trisamples.com/ TriSamples>.

!<filetable <file Adornment.ogg Adornment>>

!<h1 Concoction<date 2018.04>>

Actually an autotune remix!
Final assignment of the Coursera course <link https://www.coursera.org/learn/audio-signal-processing Audio Signal Processing for Music Applications>. The assignment requests that students collect sounds from <link https://freesound.org/ Freesound> and process them with the Python package provided in the course, carrying out operations such as spectrum separation and transposition, creating a music piece.
The original title was “Kitchraensform”. The document explains the making process; the submission package contains the audio assets and the program sources used.

!<filetable
  <file Concoction.ogg Concoction>
  <file Concoction_notes.txt Submission document>
  <file Concoction_a10-a.zip Submission package>
>

!<h1 Epigone<date 2018.06>>

Final assignment of the Coursera course <link https://coursera.org/learn/classical-composition Write Like Mozart: An Introduction to Classical Music Composition>. The assignment asks for a music piece of 20—30 bars composed for the piano and another solo instrument, adhering to a set of constraints regarding harmony and texture of the music. Dancing in shackles, to an extent.
