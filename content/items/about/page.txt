相信世界的美好可能性，永远在探索的路上；愿做一点无名的光，在黑暗的地方尽情燃烧，为万花筒般的世界添上一抹色彩。
做的事情很杂，主业设计学/互动媒体，副业计算机/作曲，正在尝试学很多很多其他的东西。INFP，无可救药的空想家，重度拖延自救中 <kao ≥﹏≤>

There are many ways of calling me, but I’m Ayu here!
Believing in the possibilities of a wonderful world, always on the way of inquiries; may I be a nameless light, burning merrily in the darkness, adding a splash of tints to this kaleidoscopic world.
I dip into a mixture of fields, majoring in design and interactive media, dabbling in computing and musical composition, while trying to stumble into too many others. INFP, incorrigibly unrealistic, trying hard to overcome procrastination <kao ≥﹏≤>
To put them up on this site is more like cherishing my own scruffy broom. A lot of content is far from perfect and I am often hesitant to call them “works”; the name sounds a bit too large. Nevertheless, I might be silently hoping that they could inspire a visitor passing by someday, or bring someone a dose of fulfillment and joy. I will be delighted if this happens.
Technically, this is a <it personal wiki>, but please call it anything you deem appropriate — a cyber home, a blog, a portfolio, a <it memex> (memory expansion). In any case, this is what it becomes in your eyes, isn’t it?
