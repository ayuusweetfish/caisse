Music from the game <link sheepdogs>. The first version received the feedback that “it was too adult-like and un-childish; expected more childishness” (also, the beginning heavily collided with the song <link https://youtu.be/c5x62ZdTmKo “开心往前飞”> from a widespread animated television series, which I had lost all memories about by then). So I went on to write the track used in the game, “Sheepdogs at Work”. The original version was put here as a bonus, “Sheepdogs on Vacation”.
