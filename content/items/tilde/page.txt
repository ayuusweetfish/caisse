<link https://cavestory.fandom.com/wiki/Soundtrack Organya> 是日本游戏设计师 <link https://studiopixel.jp/ Pixel> 制作的游戏<link https://www.cavestory.org/ 《洞窟物语》>使用的音乐格式，包含一套 16 音轨的 chiptune 合成器以及一个完善的<link https://github.com/shbow/organya 编辑工具>。
开发所用语言 <link https://www.warmplace.ru/soft/pixilang/ Pixilang> 是俄罗斯音乐人 Alexander Zolotov 设计的一门程序语言，适合用于跨平台多媒体开发。
功能已经完善，也支持大量常用编辑操作与快捷键组合。音频合成算法与原本有一定差异（采样拉伸用的是 Lanczos 滤波器，没仔细研究游戏里是什么算法），在低频打击乐器中体现较为明显，不过一时不太有动力去细调了。后来也用它做了<link zero#Heptadekka 一段长长的音乐>！

Tilde is a plain tool for music editing and playback; or a sequencer by conventional jargon.

!<img screenshot.png Screenshot>

<link https://cavestory.fandom.com/wiki/Soundtrack Organya> is the music format in the game <link https://www.cavestory.org/ Cave Story> by the Japanese designer <link https://studiopixel.jp/ Pixel>. It comprises a chiptune synthesizer with 16 tracks as well as a complete <link https://github.com/shbow/organya editor tool>.
The language it is developed in, <link https://www.warmplace.ru/soft/pixilang/ Pixilang>, is created by the Russian musician Alexander Zolotov, targeting cross-platform multimedia development.
The tool is feature-complete and supports a range of editing operations and shortcut key combinations. The audio synthesis algorithm has differences in nuances from the original game (Tilde uses the Lanczos filter for waveform stretching; did not look into the implementation of the game) that become more noticeable in lower-frequency percussion instruments, though I did not have much motivation to fine-tune it. I later made a <link zero#Heptadekka piece of music> in it.
!<img icon_large.png Icon <= small syncdark>>
The two main characters of Cave Story are both named after punctuation marks (the protagonist with the name revealed at the end, and Curly Brace). This little thing follows this convention and uses a punctuation as its name.
